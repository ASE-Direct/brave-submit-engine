How to Embed This on Your Website

To embed the BAV Savings Challenge tool on your website, simply copy and paste this code:

<iframe 
  src="https://your-deployed-url.lovable.app/" 
  width="100%" 
  height="1000px"
  style="border: none; border-radius: 8px;"
  title="BAV Savings Challenge"
  allow="clipboard-write"
></iframe>

Important: Replace https://your-deployed-url.lovable.app/ with your actual published URL from Lovable.

Responsive Embed (Recommended)

For a responsive embed that adapts to different screen sizes:

<div style="position: relative; padding-bottom: 100%; height: 0; overflow: hidden; max-width: 100%;">
  <iframe 
    src="https://your-deployed-url.lovable.app/" 
    style="position: absolute; top: 0; left: 0; width: 100%; height: 100%; border: none;"
    title="BAV Savings Challenge"
    allow="clipboard-write"
  ></iframe>
</div>
